# Assembly AI

BuildShip offers pre-configured nodes for interfacing with Assembly AI, enabling seamless integration of Assembly AI's
speech-to-text functionality within your workflows. Easily convert voice data from various sources, such as calls,
virtual meetings, and podcasts, into accurate and usable text, facilitating a range of automation and data analysis
tasks.
