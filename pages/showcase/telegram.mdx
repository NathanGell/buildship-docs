# Telegram

BuildShip provides pre-defined nodes for Telegram integration, allowing you to easily integrate Telegram's messaging
capabilities within your workflows. You can send and receive messages, manage chat groups and members, and automate
interactions, enhancing your app's communication features effortlessly.
