BuildShip provides pre-defined nodes that allow you to seamlessly store and manage your files on Google Cloud Storage.
Effortlessly integrate your workflow with Google Cloud's scalable and secure file storage solution, performing tasks
like uploading, downloading, and managing files within your own Google Cloud Storage Bucket.
