# Supabase

BuildShip provides pre-defined nodes for Supabase integration, allowing you to connect with your Supabase tables
effortlessly. This enables you to perform various actions on your data, such as querying, inserting, updating, and
deleting records, facilitating seamless data management and automation within your workflows.
