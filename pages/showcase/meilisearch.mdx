# Meilisearch

Meilisearch nodes enable seamless integration with the Meilisearch search engine, allowing users to leverage its
powerful and fast full-text search capabilities within their workflows. With these nodes, you can easily index
documents, perform searches, manage indexes, and apply filters to retrieve relevant data quickly and efficiently.
