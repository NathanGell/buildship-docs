# Fal AI

Fal AI provides pre-configured nodes to seamlessly integrate with Fal's suite of AI model APIs. This allows users to
leverage powerful pre-built AI models quickly within their applications or workflows, enabling functionalities like
natural language processing, computer vision, and predictive analytics without extensive coding.
