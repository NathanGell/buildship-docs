# Google Search Console

BuildShip offers OAuth nodes that integrate with the Google Search Console API, allowing you to easily retrieve and
manage data related to your Google Search Account. This enables you to automate tasks, extract insights from your search
performance, and optimize your website's visibility with minimal effort.
